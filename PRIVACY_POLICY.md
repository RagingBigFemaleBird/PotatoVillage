# Privacy Policy for Potato Village (土豆天天村)

**Last Updated: February 24, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Potato Village (土豆天天村), a multiplayer social deduction game ("Werewolf" / "狼人杀"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you use Potato Village, we may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

- **Nickname**: An optional display name you choose when joining a game room
- **Game Room Information**: Room numbers and seat selections you make during gameplay

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use our application, we may automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for the purpose of maintaining game sessions
- **Connection Information**: Server connection data necessary for multiplayer gameplay functionality
- **Game Session Data**: Temporary game state information required for gameplay (roles, actions, votes)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
- We do NOT collect payment or financial information
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT display advertisements

## How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

1. **Gameplay Functionality**: To enable multiplayer game sessions and synchronize game state between players
2. **Session Management**: To maintain your connection to game rooms and identify your seat/role during gameplay
3. **Display Purposes**: To show your chosen nickname to other players in the same game room

## Data Storage and Retention

- **Temporary Storage**: Game session data is stored temporarily in server memory only during active gameplay
- **No Persistent Storage**: We do not permanently store any user data. All game-related data is automatically deleted when a game session ends
- **Local Device Storage**: The app may store your preferences (such as language settings and server URL) locally on your device

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Information is only shared:

- **With Other Players**: Your nickname and in-game actions are visible to other players in the same game room as part of normal gameplay
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes

## Third-Party Services

Potato Village connects to game servers for multiplayer functionality. The app:

- Uses SignalR technology for real-time communication
- May connect to Microsoft Azure cloud services for hosting game servers
- Does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or social media services

## Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information during transmission and gameplay:

- Secure WebSocket connections for game communication
- No sensitive personal data is collected or stored
- Game sessions are isolated and data is deleted after game completion

## Children's Privacy

Potato Village is a social deduction game suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app:

- Does not require account registration
- Does not collect any personally identifiable information
- Does not contain advertisements or in-app purchases
- Does not link to social networks or external websites

Parents and guardians should supervise children's use of the application, particularly regarding interaction with other players in multiplayer game rooms.

## Permissions

The application may request the following device permissions:

- **Internet Access**: Required for connecting to game servers for multiplayer functionality
- **Vibration**: Used for haptic feedback during gameplay (optional alerts)
- **Audio**: Used for optional voiceover announcements during gameplay

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Choose not to provide optional information (such as nicknames)
- Disconnect from game sessions at any time
- Request information about what data we process (contact us at the email below)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
